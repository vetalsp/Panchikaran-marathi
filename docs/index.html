About This Text

ग्रंथपरिचय

Panchikaran (पंचीकरण) is a foundational Advaita Vedānta treatise composed by Adi Shankaracharya (c. 788–820 CE). It describes the cosmic process by which the five subtle elements (Tanmatras) combine into the five gross elements (Sthula Mahābhūtas) of which the physical universe is made — and how understanding this process reveals the identity of the individual Self with Brahman.

हा ग्रंथ पंचीकरण प्रक्रिया सांगतो — ज्या प्रक्रियेने पाच सूक्ष्म तत्त्वांचे (तन्मात्रे) पाच स्थूल महाभूते बनतात — आणि या प्रक्रियेचे ज्ञान जीव-ब्रह्म ऐक्य कसे दाखवते ते स्पष्ट करतो. श्रीमत्परमहंसपरिव्राजकाचार्य श्रीमद्शंकराचार्यांनी हा ग्रंथ रचला आहे.

This edition — Sartha Satik Panchikaran, Marathi — is a Marathi translation and detailed commentary published by Tukaram Book Depot, Mumbai (3rd edition, August 2008), in the tradition of Brahmanishtha Shriramguru. It makes the Sanskrit text accessible through thorough Marathi explanation, illustrative analogies (Dṛṣṭānta), and systematic tables (Koshtaka).
